Who Am I?

My Story

I grew up and reside in Raleigh, North Carolina and there are still parts I've never seen. I'm the oldest of three and I'm a cat person. Don't get me wrong, dogs are cute too.

One of my greatest hobbies is technology so before deciding to become a Web Designer, I worked 10+ years in technical support. I wanted to learn something new and something that allowed me to channel all my creative facets.
